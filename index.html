The founder

From goldsmith craft to a trusted gold centre.

Mr. Vijay Shahaji Bhosale began his career as a goldsmith, building his knowledge from scratch through patient, hands-on work. Over around 20 years in the business, that practical understanding of purity, weight, finishing, and customer trust became the foundation of JJ Gold Centre.

The business reflects the discipline of craft: careful checking, straightforward conversation, and respect for every customer who chooses to bring their gold for evaluation.
